# 2026 ZiPS Projections: Chicago White Sox – Comprehensive Summary
*By Dan Szymborski | November 24, 2025*

---

## **Overview**
The **ZiPS projection system** (22nd consecutive season) forecasts the **Chicago White Sox** for 2026. The team is projected to remain a **low-end talent level** squad, with **no negative depth-chart numbers** but still likely to lose **90+ games**. The focus should be on **long-term development** rather than short-term wins.

---

## **Key Takeaways**
- **2025 Performance**: Improved significantly but still **lost >100 games**. A mix of **unexpected wins** and **lackluster talent** kept them from the bottom of the league.
- **Offensive Outlook**: **One of the worst in baseball** in 2026, but **more watchable** than the 2024 roster.
- **Pitching Outlook**: **Rotation is weak** (mostly 4th/5th starters), but **bullpen depth** is a concern. **Grant Taylor** has potential as a starter.
- **Long-Term Focus**: Avoid obsession with **2026 win total**; prioritize **building a contender**.
