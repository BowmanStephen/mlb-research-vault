# Summary: Dylan Cease Blue Jays Debut — 12 Ks, Franchise Record

## Key Facts
- 12 strikeouts in 5.1 IP — Blue Jays franchise record for a debut
- 7 consecutive strikeouts — tied team record (Alex Manoah, 2021)
- 1 ER, 3 hits, 90 pitches
- Previous record: David Price (11 Ks, 2015) — Price went 9-1, 2.10 ERA in 11 starts after
- Signed $210M contract in free agency
- Debut vs Oakland Athletics, walk-off 8-7 win in 11 innings

- Facing White Sox on April 3 with dominant form
