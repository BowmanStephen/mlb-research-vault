# Summary: 2025 Rockies — Worst Team in Modern MLB History

## Key Facts
- 2025 record: 98 losses (3rd straight 100-loss season)
- Run differential: -412 (worst in modern MLB history)
- Ranked 29th in runs scored, 30th in runs allowed
- Draft WAR (2015-2025): ~10 total — fewest in MLB (Astros: 110, Cardinals: ~100)
- Kris Bryant contract: $182M for 170 games, .695 OPS, -1.6 WAR ($81M remaining)
- Bud Black fired after 7-33 start; Warren Schaeffer promoted
- Chase Dollander (2023, 9th pick): 6.52 ERA in 2025
- Attendance dropped from 6th (2019) to 15th (2025); ~75% away fans at Coors

## Notable Quotes
- Kyle Freeland: "Inside of our own ballpark, when we have 60/40, sometimes 70/30 split of fans from the other side, it eats at you a little bit."
- Matt Holliday: "I think you have to look in the mirror at three 100-loss seasons in a row."
- Walker Monfort (incoming EVP): "We're willing to look at all of that [systems, resources, organization]."

## Root Causes
1. Draft & development failures (10 WAR from top picks in a decade)
2. Front office stagnation (Dick Monfort loyalty, no outsider hires, GM Bill Schmidt)
3. Payroll inefficiency ($172M peak in 2023 with poor results)
4. Pitching development challenges (altitude)
