# Colorado Rockies — Team Profile

## 2026 Season Overview
- **Record:** 2-4 (NL West 4th)
- **Run Differential:** +3
- **Manager:** Warren Schaeffer (promoted from 3B coach mid-2025 after Bud Black fired at 7-33)

## Why They're a Fade Target
1. **2025 was historically bad:** -412 run differential, worst in modern MLB history
2. **Decade of futility:** Worst draft record in MLB (10 WAR from top 10 picks, 2015-2025)
3. **Ownership inertia:** Dick Monfort refuses outside hires; GM Bill Schmidt in place for years
4. **Coors Field illusion:** Home splits mask underlying roster weakness
5. **Key draft busts:** Riley Pint, Zac Veen, Benny Montgomery, Chase Dollander (6.52 ERA in 2025)
6. **Payroll waste:** $172M peak (2023), Kris Bryant $182M for -1.6 WAR and $81M remaining
7. **Fan exodus:** Attendance dropped from 6th (2019) to 15th (2025); ~75% away fans at Coors

## 2026 Roster Notes
- Signed Michael Lorenzen (6.23 ERA through 6 games)
- Signed Tomoyuki Sugano (1.93 ERA, limited sample)
- Kyle Freeland remains best starter (2.89 ERA)
- TJ Rumfield emerging as offensive spark (.429 BA, 214 OPS+)
- Willi Castro struggling (.130)
